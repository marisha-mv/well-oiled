WEBINAR CONTENT STRATEGY

Recommendation: Re-record your live webinar specifically for evergreen (remove all live/chat references)

Why Re-Record vs Use Live Recording:

  • Control: No "calling out attendees in chat" moments
  • Quality: Perfect lighting, audio, pacing
  • Optimization: Can A/B test different versions
  • Simplicity: No fake-live tools needed

Webinar Structure (Keep What Works):

OPENING (10 minutes): → Welcome + who this is for → Set expectations ("In next 60 min, you'll learn...") → Your story/credibility (Inc 5000 awards, 700+ clients) TEACHING (30 minutes): → The 3-step framework (or whatever you currently teach) → Case studies woven throughout → "Aha moments" that create desire STACK BUILDING (15 minutes): → "Here's how we help clients implement this..." → Module breakdown (what's included in the program) → Bonuses → Value stack ($62.5K value for $15K investment) → Testimonials (play 2-3 video testimonials) CLOSE (5 minutes): → Scarcity ("We can only take 50 clients per quarter") → Guarantee ("If you don't save 25% of your time + increase profit, full refund") → Clear CTA ("Click the button below to book your strategy call") POST-CLOSE: → "Thank you for attending. Check your email for next steps."

CRITICAL: Focus On Savings + ROI

FRAME IT AS: "If you pay $10,000 for this program and it saves you 20 hours/week... That's 1,000 hours/year. If your time is worth $200/hour (conservative for a 7-figure owner), that's $200,000 in value. You're not spending $10K. You're investing $10K to get back $200K. That's a 20X ROI." REPEAT THIS FRAME 3-4 TIMES IN THE WEBINAR

Testimonial Strategy: Show TONS of testimonials (like Warrior Babe does)

  • Weave 6-8 testimonials throughout webinar
  • Focus on RESULTS + ROI ("Saved $50K in first 90 days")
  • Video testimonials > text (more credible)
SAVINGS + ROI FRAME = EASIER CLOSE
